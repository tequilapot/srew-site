Mining Industry

S R ENGG WORKS delivers heavy-duty, reliable crushing and screening solutions designed for the demanding conditions of the mining industry. Our plants are engineered for high throughput, durability, and continuous operation, handling everything from primary extraction to material processing.

Key Solutions for Mining:

  • High-capacity Jaw Crushers for primary breaking.
  • Robust Cone Crushers for secondary and tertiary crushing.
  • Heavy-duty Vibrating Screens for precise sizing.
  • Durable Grizzly Feeders for pre-screening and feeding.
  • Efficient Conveyor Systems for material transport.

Construction & Infrastructure

We provide comprehensive solutions for the construction and infrastructure sectors, producing high-quality aggregates and manufactured sand (M-Sand) essential for roads, bridges, buildings, and other large-scale projects. Our equipment ranges from mobile units for quick deployment to stationary plants for long-term production.

Key Solutions for Construction:

  • Versatile Jaw Crushers and Cone Crushers.
  • VSI/HSI Crushers for superior M-Sand and aggregate shaping.
  • Multi-deck Vibrating Screens for accurate gradation.
  • Mobile Crushing & Screening Units for site flexibility.
  • Advanced Control Panels for plant automation.

Recycling (Construction & Demolition Waste)

S R ENGG WORKS is committed to sustainability by offering innovative C&D waste recycling plants. Our systems efficiently process construction and demolition debris, converting it into valuable resources like recycled aggregates and sand, thereby reducing landfill burden and promoting a circular economy.

Key Solutions for Recycling:

  • Integrated C&D Recycling Units.
  • Durable Jaw Crushers adapted for C&D materials.
  • Screening solutions for separating recycled products.
  • Hoppers and Conveyors for material handling.
  • Optional Trommel Screens and metal separators.

Sand Processing & Manufacturing

We specialize in equipment for manufacturing high-quality sand, including M-Sand for construction and specialized dried sand for industrial applications like foundries, glass manufacturing, and brick making. Our solutions focus on particle shape, gradation control, and moisture reduction.

Key Solutions for Sand Processing:

  • VSI/HSI Crushers for producing cubical M-Sand.
  • Efficient Sand Dryers (LPG/Gas/Diesel fired) for moisture control.
  • Vibrating Screens for precise sand classification.
  • Sand Washing Plants (components like Spiral Washers).
  • Silos for storing processed sand.
