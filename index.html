About S R ENGG WORKS

S R ENGG WORKS (SREW) is a precision engineering and industrial fabrication company, specializing in the design, manufacture, installation, and servicing of crushing, screening, sand processing, and material handling plants.

Headquartered in India, SREW serves clients across mining, construction, infrastructure, and recycling sectors with a reputation for reliability, innovation, and customization.
