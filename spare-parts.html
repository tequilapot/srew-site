Spare Parts

Genuine spare parts to keep your S R ENGG WORKS equipment running efficiently.

  1. Home
  2. Spare Parts

S R ENGG WORKS provides a comprehensive range of genuine spare parts for all our machinery. Using original spares ensures optimal performance, longevity, and reliability of your equipment, minimizing downtime and maximizing productivity.

Crusher Spares

  • Jaw plates (fixed and swing)
  • Toggle plates, toggle seats, toggle pins
  • Eccentric shafts and bearings
  • Flywheels and pitman assemblies
  • Cone liners (mantles and bowl liners)
  • Blow bars (for impact crushers)
  • Rotors and housing components
  • Cheek plates and wear liners

Screen Spares

  • Side tensioned screen mesh (various apertures and wire diameters)
  • Modular screen decks and panels
  • Screen support springs (coil and rubber)
  • Vibrating motors and drive assemblies
  • Screen body components and wear liners
  • Deck frames and clamping systems

Feeder & Hopper Spares

  • Vibratory motors for feeders
  • Feeder shafts and bearings
  • Feeder pan liners and wear-resistant bars
  • Grizzly bars (fixed and adjustable)
  • Hopper liners and structural components

Sand Dryer Spares

  • Burner heads and components (for LPG/Gas/Diesel)
  • Rotary drum lifters/paddle arms
  • Dust collector pads and filter bags
  • Insulation sheets and materials
  • Drive gears, chains, and trunnion rollers
  • Temperature sensors and control units

Conveyor Spares

  • Conveyor idlers (carrying, return, impact)
  • Conveyor rollers and bearings
  • Drive and tail pulleys
  • Conveyor belts (various plys and grades)
  • Gearboxes and motors
  • Belt scrapers and cleaners

Control Panel Spares

  • Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs)
  • Contactors and relays
  • Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)
  • Temperature control units and sensors
  • PLCs and HMI screens (if applicable)
  • Push buttons, selector switches, and indicators
